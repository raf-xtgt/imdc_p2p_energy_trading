Why Energy Trading?

Energy demand in Malaysia is increasing with growing population. The current energy infrastructure will need to be upgraded along with new ones at high costs. There would be devastating impacts on the environment if we continue using the current means of energy generation and transmission and the additional grids would make things worse.

We therefore provide an alternative to meet demands by allowing the public to trade their produced solar energy in exchange for fiat currency thus creating an incentive for the mass use of renewables.
